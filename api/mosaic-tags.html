McTagRemove

Applies proper (click) support and adds styling for use with the Material Design "cancel" icon available at https://material.io/icons/#ic_cancel.

Example:

`<mc-tag>
  <mc-icon mcTagRemove>cancel</mc-icon>
</mc-tag>`

You may use a custom icon, but you may need to override the mc-tag-remove positioning styles to properly center the icon within the tag.
